Jackson Heights



Jackson Heights is located in Queens. It is surrounded by Woodside on the western side, Corona on the eastern side, East Elmhurst and Astoria Heights on the northern side, and Elmhurst on the southern side. The main part of Jackson Heights that this website focuses on is the southerstern side. If you zoom into the map below, you will see a train station: "Roosevelt Av - Jackson Heights," which is in the area that we will focus on. Have fun!
