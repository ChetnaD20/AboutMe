Ethnic Wear

Ethnic clothes are the heart of Indian culture. There is something different to wear at different festivals. There are so many types of clothes that come from all corners of India. As you look through the pictures, I know you're going to think about how beautiful the clothes are. The real best place to buy them is online or in India because these stores mark up the prices to get high profits. However, if you can bargain like an Indian, you're sure to get a good price! Also, most of the pictures show clothes for women because there are so many more varieties for women than men.

With every outfit comes jewelry! In old times, Indian families used gold to show how wealthy they are. That jewelry would pass down to the next generation and the next so on so forth. Today, not as many families buy gold, but there are still a lot.
